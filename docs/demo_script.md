# ResQAI Demo Video Script

**Target Length:** 2 Minutes
**Tone:** Professional, Urgent, Innovative.

---

### Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
**Visual:** Show Title Slide ("ResQAI").
**Audio:**
"In a disaster, data isn't the problem. Making sense of it is. Hundreds of distress calls, tweets, and sensor alerts flood in simultaneously. Current systems just put dots on a map. They don't coordinate the rescue. Today, we present ResQAI."

### Scene 2: The Dashboard & Ingestion (0:30 - 1:00)
**Visual:** Switch to Streamlit Dashboard. Show the "Active Incidents" list.
**Action:** Type a new emergency into the 'Simulate New Incident' box (e.g., "School roof collapse, multiple injuries"). Click 'Broadcast'.
**Audio:**
"Here is the ResQAI Command Center. As you can see, we have a live feed of incidents. Let's simulate a new distress signal coming in from a collapsed school. As soon as the data hits, our Multi-Modal Ingestion engine extracts the location and urgency, updating the map instantly."

### Scene 3: The AI Planner (1:00 - 1:30)
**Visual:** Focus on the "Generative Response Planner" section. Click the "Generate Action Plan" button.
**Audio:**
"Now, this is where ResQAI differs from a standard dashboard. We don't just watch. We act. By clicking 'Generate Plan', our LLM analyzes the entire Knowledge Graph—understanding dependencies between the flood waters and the trapped victims."

### Scene 4: The Result (1:30 - 2:00)
**Visual:** Show the generated JSON plan / Action Cards (Team Assignments).
**Audio:**
"In seconds, it produces a strategic allocation plan. It dispatches the nearest Medical Unit to the school and routes the Drone Squad to scan for safe paths. This is the future of disaster response: Faster, Smarter, and Life-Saving. Thank you."
